open a terminal emulator and then run the command `gpg --import prot`
(or whatever equivalent for your interface/context). If you are doing
this inside of Emacs, then mark the entire key, including the complete
`BEGIN` and `END` lines and invoke the command `epa-import-keys-region`.

If you are going to contact me, make sure to **send me your public
key**, else I will not be able to reply to you: <https://protesilaos.com/contact>.
