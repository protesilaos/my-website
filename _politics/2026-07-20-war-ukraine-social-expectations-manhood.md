the Zelenskyy regime, in the form of diplomatic support and financial
aid.

essential operational expenses and the remainder is siphoned back to

the European taxpayers are subsidising the European oligarchs while
consigning Ukrainians to the meatgrinder.

The EU-UK tandem does not have a vision for how this war will end,
other than Russian capitulation---good luck doing what the military
juggernauts of Napoleon and the Third Reich failed miserably at!

Russia is clearly winning this war on the ground. They are making slow
yet steady advances in the Donbas region while terrorising other parts
of the country. Those gains on the frontier occur at what may seem as
snail's pace because this is the nature of modern warfare: when
virtually every apartment can be used as a launching pad for drones,
you cannot conduct a blitzkrieg.

Western media wants to create a different impression by exaggerating
the impact Ukrainian drone strikes on Russian territory have on the
dynamics of the war. Starting fires in civilian infrastructure does
not change the realities on the battlefield where Ukraine's frontlines
are falling apart.

The narrative that Ukraine is outsmarting the putative dumb Russians
through sheer genius and technical acumen is designed to keep the war
on the agenda. At times it feels like Ursula and Kaja only say
"Russia, Russia, Russia". This is beneficial for industrialists and,
by extension, financiers who understand warfare as a means of
regaining Europe's lost competitiveness in global markets.

European elites are aware of what is happening in Ukrainian society,

Havryshko.

Looking at the future, Westerners have not been sincere in their

expansion). This means that the Russian regime will likely be
emboldened to continue its campaign further south to capture all of
coastal Ukraine and potentially connect with Transnistria. They will
stop when they want, which is catastrophic for the people of Ukraine
and madness for the rest of us.

Yes, this is also costly for Russia. Even the winners of a war, or
dominant powers in general, pay a hefty price for their supremacy.
Just check the American public debt to get a sense of what that looks
like and what sort of odious burden it is for future generations.

## Manhood and public pressure

The war shall continue and the poor will keep paying for it with blood
and materials. Such are the structures of power in Europe, Ukraine,
and beyond, and such is the fate of the peoples who do not control
their own politics.

Shifting gears, there is a wider theme about the role of men in
society. Taking another quote out of Marta Havryshko's article (the
parenthetic explanation is mine):

> This asymmetry [of those who do not fight the war and are shielded
> from its consequences publicly shaming men who dodge the draft] lies
> at the heart of growing social tensions. Those who champion moral
> condemnation often do so from positions of relative safety and
> freedom. They can cross borders, pursue careers abroad, and retain
> control over their own lives. The men they shame cannot. From the
> very first days of the war, Ukraine has cultivated the image of the
> ideal man: self-sacrificing, strong, courageous, and unfailingly
> committed to defending his family, his home, his nation. He is
> prepared, if necessary, to give his life for this cause.

This is an age-old view of manhood which is partially based on
biological fact. The average man is substantially stronger and more
physically dominant than the average woman. Where biology ends and
ideology starts is on the normative implications of this basic
reality: what men _ought_ to do and how they _ought_ to feel. That is
a matter of culture or opinion more broadly.

Every person needs structure in their life: a framework to keep them
present in day-to-day realities and to maintain their readiness for
the relevant rigours; an arrangement that helps them direct their life
force towards some useful project. Without structure, people are lost.
They become wasteful in their actions and self-destructive in their
habits.

Structure in life either comes from within, in the form of discipline,
or from the social milieu as peer pressure. Discipline, especially the
strict kind, is the rare condition, so peer pressure becomes intense
and ubiquitous. In our case here, males are expected to do manly
activities as determined by culture, such as to serve and to protect
and to sacrifice themselves.

The average man needs an outlet for his high capacity for aggression.
This will either be some socially agreed upon convention, which has a
benign effect on the collective, like work or sport or gaming that is
done in moderation, or it shall be expressed as some anti-social form
of violence, criminality, terrorism, or self-harm. In plain terms, the
good man is the busy man; the best man is the committed man.

All of this covers the positive side of ideology that is layered upon
the basic reality of masculine biology: what men _ought_ to do to keep
society going.

The negative side occurs once emotional manipulation enters the mix to
channel man's power towards questionable ends. The average man is
highly susceptible to questions of his masculinity. That is his weak
point: you shout at him, he shouts back, you punch him, he punches
back, you doubt he is man enough and he feels something break inside
of him. He becomes insecure as soon as you raise doubts about his
ability to perform, where performance is once again a cultural
construct.

Women who understand this are those who control and micromanage the
relationship even in a superficially patriarchical setting. And men
who understand this tend to become Internet famous masculinity
merchants who belittle other men in order to convert them to paying
customers.

Emotional manipulation is how you take a healthy person and convince
them that they are fundamentally lacking in some ways which they can
only remedy if they take such and such product. Once you brainwash a
man to think of himself as a man manqué, you can make him do whatever
you want.

It is no coincidence, then, that the public shaming of men in Ukraine
is done by plenty of women. This is a super effective form of

Both in its positive and negative applications, structure is a matter
of control. When public opprobrium happens, it is to enforce the

look for are the ideocentric underpinnings of the prevailing opinions.
Those are never mere biology, even when they are formed around a

The power apparatus wants to control men at all costs so as to secure
