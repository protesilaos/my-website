I am lowering the price of my coaching services to 10 EUR per hour.
Effective immediately.

This is because of the prevailing economic conditions, which I suspect
make it harder for prospective students to justify the old rate of 20
EUR per hour (was 15 EUR for college students).

I will inform current students who may not read this about the lowered
price point.

I thank all those who have taken up my coaching offer. Take care!
