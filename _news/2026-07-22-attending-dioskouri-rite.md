When you act, strange things happen. Yesterday I wrote [Religion and
theology in light of the Greek gods](https://protesilaos.com/politics/2026-07-21-religion-theology-greek-gods/)
and now I have the chance to meet relevant people.

This Sunday I will join a group of Greek polytheists (ethnic Greek
religion) from Cyprus in a rite related to Dioskouri at a temple
overlooking the sea.

My immediate plan is to not record anything from the event, out of
respect for other people's privacy. Though I might take a selfie.

My long-term objective is to form a tightly knit community or
otherwise join and contribute to an existing one.

We are what we do, not what we claim.
