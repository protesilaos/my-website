---
title: "Poem: From the age of myth"
excerpt: "Just read the poem. No further comment."
---

```
From the age of myth
you arrive as thunder
and whispering fountain
As the vivifying warmth
of a luminous star
Intensity you are
of a special kind
that moves hearts
and reshapes the land
In the dragon's year
grey became blue
green turned to amber
Indeterminate
Captivating
Potent
Manifest you now make
the collective will
of the sages
in yet another
awakening
```
