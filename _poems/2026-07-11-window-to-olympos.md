---
title: "Poem: Window to Olympos"
excerpt: "Just read the poem. No further comment."
---

```
Open a window
to sunbathed Olympos
where three eagles
guide mountaineers
to the slopes of serenity
Pause and marvel
as the doves take flight
from the ancient oak tree
to reach the golden grapevines
that bee-eaters
now call home
```
