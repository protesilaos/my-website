---
title: "Poem: The echo of my steel"
excerpt: "Just read the poem. No further comment."
---

```
Where aromatic rose
and blackthorn shrub
spring from shared soil
you find what you seek
once you realise
that the mountain-god
heeds my prayer
as the echo
of my steel
```

I wrote the poem in the context of this journal entry: [Doing what I
must](https://protesilaos.com/commentary/2026-04-29-doing-what-i-must/).
