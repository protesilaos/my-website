---
title: "Poem: The November skies"
excerpt: "Just read the poem. No further comment."
---

```
Set ideologies aside
The flocking crows
shall guide you
to look skyward
where the travelling Sun
paints its November skies
```

I wrote this in the context of my journal entry [Slowing down and appreciating the little things](https://protesilaos.com/commentary/2025-11-05-slowing-down-appreciating-little-things/) (2025-11-05).
