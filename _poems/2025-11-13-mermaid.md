title: "Poem: Mermaid"
excerpt: "Just read the poem. No further comment."

```
It is Alexander's sister
who emerges from the seabed
to warn wanton sailors
that exotic landscapes
shall not free them
from the ocean's spell
```

I wrote this as a conclusion to the essay [Writing to refine my ideas and discover new ones](https://protesilaos.com/commentary/2025-11-13-write-refine-discover-ideas/) (2025-11-13).
