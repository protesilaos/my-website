title: "Poem: Eagle"
excerpt: "Just read the poem or listen to it.  No further comment."
---

```
Mountain eagle
Peerless among those clouds
How is loneliness?
```

I encounter eagles regularly.  This morning I saw another.  I was
inspired to compose this and record it on the spot.
