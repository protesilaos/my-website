---
title: "Poem: Frogs on a summer night"
excerpt: "Just read the poem. No further comment."
---

```
On this summer night
frogs appear
to retell the story
of days gone by
In water they stay
yet never ride the waves
to the farthest reaches
where fire and ice
are one
Here they reside
a creek as their habitat
Content with what they have
they admire the fig trees
which shall nourish
the children of the falcon
```
