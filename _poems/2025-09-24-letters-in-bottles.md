**UPDATE 2025-09-25 13:51 +0300:** The original used "words..in
bottles" and I have now changed it to "letters...in bottles".

```
Continue
to put letters
in bottles
and toss them
to the waves
until
years later
on second read
you realise
your messages
were being sent
```
